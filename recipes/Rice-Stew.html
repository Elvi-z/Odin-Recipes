Jollof Rice



Nigerian Jollof Rice and Stew is a rich and incredibly tasty west African two-pot Meal. It is a very versatile dish, and it is usually made from scratch using Rice, Tomatoes, Pimento peppers, Tomato paste, Scotch bonnet, Onions, Salt, and other spices.


Ingridients for preparing Rice and Stew

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes tin or fresh
  • Curry Powder

  • Nutmeg
  • Red Pepper
  • Onions
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Already boiled meat/Chicken
  • Crayfish
  • Spices

Steps in preparing Rice and Stew

  1. Add your pepper, Tomatoes(if fresh) into a blender
  2. Blend to paste
  3. Add the rice into a boiling pot of water, boil for 10mins and sieve then cook till desired texture
  4. Dry a pot in fire and add Vegetable Oil and allow to heat
  5. Slice Onion bulb and pour into hot Oil
  6. Add blended paste
  7. Stir until paste is rid of water and has only oil
  8. Add seasoning, crayfish, meat, and spices plus salt
  9. Add water to satisfaction, hot water
  10. allow to boil
